All you really need to know is that we get hungry

...a lot.

We know what it feels like. Feeling your wallet cry out in pain when you see that an Uber to Ktown costs $25. Being so hungry at 2AM that you’d sell a kidney for some food. Smelling ramen from the neighboring suite and wishing you had some… People need snacks. But not another pack of off-brand pita chips…we want the snacks that remind us of home.


So, we founded Wanwan! Right now, we’re still small, but if you stick with us, we hope we can bring more and more snacks to your door, faster.
